Dog Walking

Your dog gets a walk around your neighborhood. Perfect for busy days and dogs with extra energy to burn. Meet your dog's perfect match before you book. Request a free in-person Meet & Greet. Choose the days of the week and times your dog needs a walk to keep your pup on a schedule.

Pet Daycare

Your beloved pet spends the day with us. Drop them off in the morning and pick up a happy pup in the evening. They’ll be treated like part of the family in a comfortable environment and will get all the attention they need.

Spa & Grooming

We are specialized in creating custom grooming styles to suit your pet’s unique personality and breed. We can create anything from a traditional breed cut to a more creative, artistic style. This service includes a bath, blow-dry, brush-out, nail trim, ear cleaning, haircut and teeth brushing.

Cat Sitting

No more cages, kennels, growls, or yowls. Allow us to bring the purrfect hospitality to you through our cat sitters. Your cat's safety and well-being is at the heart of what we do.

Pet Training

Dog training classes are a great way to socialise your dog and meet other dog owners. Whether your puppy is a beginner, or you have an older dog who needs a refresher class, our trainers have the solution for you.

Pet Medicine

Our goal is to provide the most accurate, reliable, up-to-date pet health information to help you navigate the everyday ups and downs of pet parenting. As a pet parent, you deserve to have access to the tools, tips, and insights you need to keep your pets healthy.
